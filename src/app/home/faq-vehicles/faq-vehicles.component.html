Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  What is Car Nation Subscribe?
Susbcription by CarNation is a 3, 6, 12, 18, 24 & 36 months subscription program with an all-inclusive monthly payment. This payment includes the usage of the vehicle, maintenance, and other common services related to vehicle ownership. The offering will be fulfilled by a third party leasing company which will also take care of the insurance coverage, Road Side Assistance & disallowance.

  For how long can I subscribe a car?
Our Subscription Tenures are flexible for you to Subscribe for a period of 3, 6, 12, 18, 24 & 36 months.

  Can I extend my tenure?
If you decide to extend the Subscription period while you are using the car, you will need to give prior written request of 30 days to leasing partner for the extension.Upon receipt of the request, leasing partner shall ascertain possibility of extension and communicate to you within 7 days of request. If leasing partner confirms the availability of the Vehicle for aforesaid extension, then you shall be required to pay revised reduced Subscription Fee as advised by leasing partner for the Additional Term.

  How long will it take to get the car?
The subscribed car delivery usually takes 15 days from the time of booking, subject to timely collection of documents and verification and subject to availability. Subscribers are kept informed at all the stages in the case of any changes or unforseen delays.
